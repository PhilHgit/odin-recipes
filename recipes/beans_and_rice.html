Beans and Rice (savory classic)

Description

Amazing Beans and Rice recipe, this will make all your friends say ohhhhh ahhhhhh. Totally vegan and packed full of clean carbs and protein prepare to feast in the this plant based glory without feeling any shame or remorse what so ever.

Ingredients

  • Rice. Long-grain
  • Veggie Stock
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Jalapeno
  • Beans
  • Spices
  • Cilantro

Preparation

  1. Cook the Rice.
      Cook 1 cup of white or brown rice according to the package directions. While the rice is cooking, you can begin preparing the beans.
  2. Saut√© the Aromatics.
      3. In a separate pot or skillet, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Add one diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add two minced garlic cloves and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Simmer the Beans
      4. Add one 15-ounce can of rinsed black or pinto beans to the pot with the onions and garlic. Stir in 1/2 cup of broth or water and your preferred seasonings (e.g., cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper). Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the liquid has thickened.
  4. Combine and Serve
      5. Spoon the finished beans over the cooked rice. Garnish with optional toppings like fresh cilantro, shredded cheese, or a dollop of sour cream.
